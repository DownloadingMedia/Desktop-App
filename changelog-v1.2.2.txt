DownloadMedia v1.2.2

- Resolved first-attempt yt-dlp timeout errors on fresh device starts.

- Checkboxes now have shrink-0 styles globally to prevent distorting.
- Socket Timeout options in Settings default to 30s for slower connections.

Reliability:
- Provided robust process timeouts during initial metadata fetch.
