DownloadMedia v1.0.0 - Production Release

What is DownloadMedia? (Core Features)
DownloadMedia is a powerful, native desktop media downloader designed to extract audio and video from the internet at maximum speed with zero ads or throttling.

Release highlights:
- First official production release of DownloadMedia!
- Made tooltips for "Supported Platforms" fully responsive and bounds-clamped to prevent overlapping the sidebar or clipping off-screen.
- Added automatic taskbar theme-adaptive color inversion for thumbnail toolbar control buttons (icons automatically toggle between white and black depending on the Windows light/dark theme).
- Fixed player controls layout by rendering a mathematically mirrored forward skip button to match the backward skip button perfectly.
- Cleaned up player volume slider styles to render in crisp white (both fill and thumb) across Chromium/Electron.
- Fixed encoding issues where middle dots rendered as Â·.
- Safe update downloader implementation with auto-cleanup of cache folders.

Features:
- Universal Platform Support: Download content from over 20+ platforms including YouTube, Instagram, TikTok (No Watermark), Twitter/X, Facebook, Reddit, Vimeo, SoundCloud, and Twitch.
- Extreme Quality (Up to 8K): Extract videos in their absolute highest native quality (4320p 8K, 2160p 4K, 1440p 2K, and 1080p HD). 
- Audio Extraction: Convert any video directly to crystal-clear audio formats including 320kbps MP3, FLAC, WAV, and M4A.
- Multi-Threaded Engine: Advanced multi-part downloading pipelines powered by native binaries (yt-dlp/ffmpeg) to max out your internet connection.
- Built-in Queue Manager: Track multiple downloads at once. See exactly which file is currently downloading, active transfer speeds, total file sizes, and estimated time remaining.
- Zero Bloatware & Ad-Free: 100% free forever. No premium tiers, no hidden paywalls, and absolutely no ads inside the application.
- Built-in Media Player: Watch and preview your downloaded media directly inside the app.
